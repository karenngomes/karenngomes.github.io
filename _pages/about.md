I'm a Master's student in Computer Science at the Federal University of Minas Gerais (UFMG), where I work with the [LoCuS](https://dcc.ufmg.br/laboratorio-de-computacao-social/) (Social Computing Laboratory) advised by [Fabrício Benevenuto](https://homepages.dcc.ufmg.br/~fabricio/). I study how harmful content spreads and is regulated online, including toxic discourse against public figures on social media, misinformation, and the online betting ecosystem in Brazil.
